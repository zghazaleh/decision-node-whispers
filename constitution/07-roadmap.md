# 07 — Roadmap (Vision, not Implementation)

This is the long-term arc of the product. Dates are deliberately omitted; sequence and shape are what matter.

```
Current MVP
   ↓
Curated archive
   ↓
Decision Profile
   ↓
Archive (browsable, searchable, returnable)
   ↓
Creator platform
   ↓
Community publishing
   ↓
Forks
   ↓
Institutions
   ↓
Decision Graph
   ↓
The world's archive of consequential decisions
```

## Current MVP

A small set of authored Decision Nodes, the Director, the Analyzer, and a per-case local save. Proves the loop.

## Curated archive

A growing set of in-house Decision Nodes spanning categories — corporate, legal, civic, aerospace, personal. Curated by editors, not crowdsourced yet. Establishes a tonal baseline community work will later be measured against.

## Decision Profile

A cross-case, longitudinal portrait of the player's reasoning patterns. Not a score; a description. The profile is the answer to "what did playing twelve cases reveal about how I think?" — and is visible only to the player.

## Archive

The case set becomes browsable, searchable, returnable. Players can revisit a case after a year and see how their reasoning has shifted. Old saves are preserved as artifacts.

## Creator platform

Tooling for new authors to write a Decision Node end-to-end — canon, characters, archetypes, presets, scene. The platform enforces the constitution structurally: a case cannot publish if it lacks hidden knowledge, conflicting incentives, or an authored consequence timeline.

## Community publishing

Authored cases from outside contributors enter the archive under a clear editorial standard. Attribution is first-class. Versioning is first-class.

## Forks

A case can be forked: another author takes the same dilemma and authors a different set of archetypes, a different canon, a different opening. Forks are an editorial form, not a competition.

## Institutions

Universities, law firms, medical residencies, leadership programs author private Decision Nodes for their own people. The product becomes infrastructure for any field that trains judgment under uncertainty.

## Decision Graph

Across thousands of cases and millions of sessions, patterns emerge — recurring archetypes, recurring blind spots, recurring tensions. The Decision Graph is a public, anonymized atlas of how people reason. It is research, not a leaderboard.

## The world's archive of consequential decisions

The end state is a permanent, public, searchable library of difficult human decisions — authored, played, reflected upon, preserved. Decision Node becomes the place this kind of thinking lives.

## What is *not* on the roadmap

- Multiplayer competitive modes.
- Live-ops events, daily challenges, streaks.
- Cosmetic progression, currencies, marketplaces.
- Decision-distribution stats shown to players.
- "Recommended decision" overlays.
- AI-authored cases at scale without editorial review.

These are not deferred. They are excluded.
