# Decision Nodes — Content & Virality Strategy

## Core Principle
Don't sell the product. Sell the feeling. The experience is 20-40 minutes; the hook is 15 seconds. These are different jobs — design for both separately.

## The Flywheel
Dilemma cards drive people to the product → they play → they get a reading → they share the reading → new people see the reading → they're curious what theirs would say → they click the dilemma card → loop.

Each content type feeds the next. Start at the top, don't wait for the bottom to be ready.

## Three Content Types

### Phase 1 — Dilemma Cards (Zero build required. Ships in a day.)
Text on black. Cinematic. No voiceover. Pure pressure.

Format: 9:16 vertical, 10-15 seconds, atmospheric audio from the existing case library.

Example:
> "You're a mother at a freezing checkpoint. One permit. Two children. One crosses tonight. Pick."
> — cut to black — decision-nodes.com

**5 cases to start with:** The Checkpoint, The Glitch, The Rope, The Release, Black Site.

Test all 5. Let the data pick which hooks land hardest. The ones that spike become the ad creative.

### Phase 2 — Results Reveals (After streaming text build)
The post-decision reading is Spotify Wrapped for decision-making. 8 cognitive scores, a personalized psychological portrait, a line like "your blind spot is overconfidence." People share that.

**Required before filming:**
1. Stream the reading word by word — turns static result into a moment with natural tension
2. Visual container must hold at 9:16 — typography, spacing, dark aesthetic

Once done: play a case, screen-record the reading appearing, add minimal caption. Viral mechanic: "it said this about me, what would it say about you?"

### Phase 3 — POV Format (After mobile UX polished)
"POV: You have 30 seconds to decide which child's name to give the border guard."

Show the opening. The pressure. The decision. The result. 45-60 seconds. Requires editorial judgment. Do after Phase 2 validates the concept.

## Platform Angles
- **TikTok / Instagram Reels** — Results reveals and POV. Hook in first 2 seconds. Personal and emotional.
- **X / Twitter** — Quote the reading. Literary one-liners from the AI analysis. One line, black card, no context.
- **LinkedIn** — Decision science angle. High share rate for self-insight content.
- **YouTube Shorts** — Slightly longer. Case walkthroughs. Good for the interactive fiction audience.

## Audience Seeding
Seed 3-5 community accounts before going wide:
1. Philosophy/ethics Twitter/TikTok
2. Psychology/self-insight communities
3. Interactive fiction / literary gaming (Her Story, Disco Elysium fans)

Organic reactions from trusted voices outperform produced content.

## Sequencing

| Results Reveals | Streaming text build | 3-5 days | Personal identity sharing |
