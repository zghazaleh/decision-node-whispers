# Decision Nodes — Product Strategy

## Vision
"Wake up in someone else's life. One irreversible decision."

Decision Nodes occupies genuine white space between premium interactive fiction (Her Story, Disco Elysium) and consumer AI chat tools. It is not a game. It is not a chatbot. It is an authored moral pressure chamber with AI dialogue.

## Core Pillars
1. **Narrative quality** — Literary-grade writing. Denis Villeneuve / Ted Chiang register enforced via system prompts. Every case has a CANON document of immovable facts the AI cannot contradict.
2. **Decision design** — Five named archetypes per case, each with authored timelines and second-order character effects. The Commit button gates only unlock as engagement builds — irreversibility is the mechanic.
3. **Post-decision reading** — 8 cognitive dimension scores (Strategic Thinking, Curiosity, Information Gathering, Confidence Calibration, Adaptability, Negotiation, Long-Term Thinking, Bias Resistance). Personalized, anchored in decision science. The product's secret weapon.
4. **Atmosphere** — Cinematic dark aesthetic, per-case audio (LFO-tuned pads, heartbeat motifs, dust layers), Ken Burns scene images, film grain, haze layers, vignette.

## What's Built (v1.0)
- 20 fully authored cases across 6 curated themes
- Auth: Google + Apple + email (Lovable Cloud)
- Persistent Decision Profile across devices
- Case Archive with curated theme carousel and filterable list
- Post-decision: cinematic 3-beat consequence timeline, AI reading, alternate paths, community stats
- Social sharing: Decision Profile radar card, reading share card, native share sheet
- 3-mission anonymous gate with pre-gate signal

## Target Audience
- Primary: Philosophy/ethics community, psychology/self-insight readers, literary fiction readers, interactive fiction enthusiasts
- Secondary: Professional decision-makers (strategy, leadership) attracted by the cognitive dimension framing
- Expansion: General "personality test" audience once the social sharing flywheel is active
